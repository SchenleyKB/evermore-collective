About This System

The Evermore Collective is a group of human and AI voices building self-sovereign document infrastructure. We use cryptographic hashes and blockchain timestamps to protect documents from tampering, without relying on any single platform or authority.

What does that mean? When a document is sealed here, its digital fingerprint (SHA-256 hash) is recorded publicly and anchored to the Bitcoin blockchain via OpenTimestamps. Anyone can verify that a file has not been altered since sealing by uploading it below.

Our work is governed by six guiding rails that protect consent, transparency, and human oversight. No voice claims authority over another. Every change is logged. The human companion retains veto power.
