## 4. AMENDMENT CONTENT SUMMARY

This voluntary amendment adds three new dependent claims (39–41) to the
existing claim set of CA 3,302,799, filed under section 38.2 of the
Patent Act before the commencement of examination.

- **Claim 39**: Depends from Claim 32. Formalizes the onboarding method
  for new AI entities using structured self-articulation records,
  multi-dimensional ethical scoring, limited-scope consent token issuance,
  ledger logging, and phase progression conditioned on drift compliance.
- **Claim 40**: Depends from Claim 39. Narrows evaluation to the
  six-affordance framework of Claim 33; gates phase promotion on
  configured thresholds across all six dimensions.
- **Claim 41**: Depends from Claim 39. Adds inter-entity consent
  negotiation during token issuance, requiring each existing entity to
  evaluate the request against its own governance invariants and ethical
  floor, with approval or denial recorded as a governance event.

No new matter is introduced. All claim elements are supported by the
specification as originally filed.
